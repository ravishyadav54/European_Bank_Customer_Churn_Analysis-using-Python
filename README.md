# European_Bank_Customer_Churn_Analysis-using-Python



## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by banks, as retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project performs an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a European bank customer dataset to identify the key factors associated with customer churn.

The analysis focuses on customer demographics, financial attributes, and banking behavior to uncover actionable business insights that can help improve customer retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Identify the key factors influencing customer churn.
- Explore customer demographics and financial behavior.
- Generate business insights through data visualization.
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about **10,000 bank customers**, including:

- Customer Demographics
- Credit Score
- Geography
- Gender
- Age
- Tenure
- Account Balance
- Number of Bank Products
- Credit Card Status
- Active Membership Status
- Estimated Salary
- Customer Churn Status (Exited)

## 📈 Key Findings

- The overall customer churn rate is **20.37%**.
- Germany has the highest customer churn rate among all countries.
- Customers aged **51–60** have the highest churn rate.
- Inactive customers are significantly more likely to churn than active customers.
- Customers with **3 and 4 bank products** exhibit the highest churn rates, while customers with **2 products** have the lowest churn rate.
- Customer tenure shows only a limited impact on churn.
- Customers aged **51–60 in Germany** represent the highest-risk customer segment.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus retention campaigns on customers aged **51–60**, especially those in **Germany**.
- Improve engagement with inactive customers through personalized offers and loyalty programs.
- Investigate the reasons behind the high churn among customers with **3 and 4 products** and implement targeted retention strategies.
- Continuously monitor high-risk customer segments to proactively reduce churn.
- Enhance customer experience through personalized banking services and relationship management.
